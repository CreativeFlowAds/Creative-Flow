About Us

Meet the team behind Creative Flow Advertising and discover our mission to empower Durango businesses.

Our Mission

The driving force behind everything we do

Empowering Local Businesses

At Creative Flow Advertising, our mission is to empower Durango's local businesses by leveraging the transformative power of AI, making cutting-edge tools accessible to simplify operations, improve efficiency, and unlock growth potential.

We believe that every business, regardless of size, deserves access to the most innovative marketing strategies and technologies available. By bridging the gap between advanced AI solutions and local entrepreneurship, we help our community thrive in an increasingly digital marketplace.

Meet Our Team

The creative minds behind Creative Flow Advertising

Kahlil

Co-Founder & Marketing Strategist

Kahlil's name means "trustworthy or loyal friend," a trait he embodies both personally and professionally. Embracing the uniqueness of his name, Kahlil strives to bring a sense of friendship and loyalty to his work, aiming to assist family-owned and local businesses in Durango that may be lagging in marketing.

He believes every business deserves to showcase their craft, art, or service, and he is dedicated to helping them shine a light on what they hold dearest.

Wes

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Born and raised in Colorado, Wes is a Fort Lewis College graduate with a passion for adventure and storytelling. His diverse experiences range from leading wilderness expeditions to capturing moments in the Himalayas.

As an educator, digital marketer, and creative storyteller, Wes aims to inspire growth and authentic connections. He believes every story deserves to be told with purpose and passion, and through education, media, and outdoor exploration, he empowers others to share their unique journeys.
