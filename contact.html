Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us

What can I expect after I submit the contact form?

After submitting the form, you'll receive an automated confirmation email immediately. Our team will then review your inquiry and respond within 1 business day to schedule a consultation or answer your questions.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes! We offer both in-person meetings for Durango-based clients and virtual consultations via Zoom or Google Meet for clients anywhere. Our virtual consultations are just as effective and comprehensive as our in-person meetings.

Is there a cost for initial consultations?

No, we offer complimentary 30-minute initial consultations to discuss your business needs and determine if we're a good fit for each other. During this consultation, we'll learn about your business, answer your questions, and provide an overview of how we might be able to help.

How soon can we start working together?

Our typical onboarding process takes 1-2 weeks from the initial consultation to beginning work. This includes completing an in-depth business assessment, finalizing the scope of work, and preparing your customized strategy. For urgent needs, we may be able to expedite this process.
